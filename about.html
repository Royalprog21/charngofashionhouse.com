Our Story

About Charngo

25+ years of crafting quality, decent attire for every walk of life — right here in Nairobi.

Who We Are

A Nairobi Original

Charngo Fashion House is a tailoring and design company based and founded in Kenya by Charles Obiero. We pride in providing and delivering decent attire for all occasions and all walks of life.

Where to Find Us

Visit Our Workshop

We are located in Umoja 2 Market, Nairobi, Kenya. Our service delivery is nationwide.

Our Drive

Fashion for Everyone

In a generation where fashion and trends are ever-changing, we aspire to keep you in the loop with the latest designs and make you look great even on a budget.

We believe that decency, class and elegance is not a far-fetched reality — and with our skilled experts, we deliver as per your needs.

What We Do

All Occasions Covered

We design and tailor outfits for all occasions including weddings, funerals, corporate events, casual wear, and school uniforms. We also offer fashion consultancy, handle institutional tenders for schools and corporates, and provide training for students and upcoming tailors.

Our Heritage

25+ Years of Excellence

Charngo Fashion House has built its brand for over 25 years, delivering quality and timely services. With decades of experience, we have built trust and brought satisfaction to many clients and students who have interned with us. Our expert has mentored many, and through apprenticeship their knowledge has grown into unmatched skills in the industry.

Beyond Tailoring

Other Services

We offer much more than sewing — from consultations to tenders to training.

Tailoring & Design Services

  • Custom Design Consultations: Work with our expert designers to create unique outfits tailored to your style and preferences.
  • Alterations & Repairs: Professional alteration and repair services to ensure your garments fit perfectly.
  • Material Sourcing: We help you find the perfect fabrics and materials, ensuring quality and style.
  • Style Recommendations: Personalized style advice to choose outfits that suit your body type and occasion.
  • Bulk Orders for Events: Weddings, corporate events, or special occasions — we handle bulk orders with care.

Institutional Tenders

We supply uniforms and attire under tender for:

  • School uniforms
  • Corporate wear
  • Event wear
  • Sports wear

Training & Workshops

We offer flexible, hands-on training classes and workshops for people interested in learning and bettering their skills in fashion and design. Our programs are tailored to different skill levels — from beginners to advanced practitioners.
