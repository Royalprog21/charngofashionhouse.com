Who we are

Charngo Fashion House is tailoging and design company based and founded in Kenya by Charles Obiero. We pride in prividing and delivering decent attire for all occasions and all walks of life.

Where to find us

We are located in Umoja 2 market, Nairobi Kenya and our service delivery is nationwide.

What is our drive

In a generation where fashion and trend is ever changing, we aspire to keep you in the loop with the latest designs and make you look good even whn you are on a budget.
We believe that decency, class and elegance is not a far fetch reality, and with our skilled and experienced experts, we deliver as per your needs.

What do we do

We design and tailor outfits for all occasions including but not limited to: weddings, funerals, corporate events, casual wear, school uniforms among others. We also offer fashion consultancy services to individuals and groups.
We also do tenders for schools, corporates and many other institutions. We also do trainings for students and upcoming tailors.

How long

Charngo Fashion House has builded its brand for over 25 years now, delivering quality and timely services to our clients. With the years of experinence, we have builded trust and brought satisfaction to many clients and students who have interned with us. Our expert has mentored many and through apprenticeship, their knowledge has developed into unmatched skills in the industry.
